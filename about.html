Where I Grew Up

I was born in Mexico City, where I lived for the first 12 years of my life. When I was entering 7th grade I moved to Pullman, Washington, a small town in the PNW where I stayed until my last year of highschool. Now I live in Boston. Hobbies of mine

During the pandemic, I discovered a lot of new hobbies, such as knitting, ceramics, wood working, building. It made me understand that spending time doing something productive with my time, not just looking at a screen, was helping my mental health and strengthening the realtionships I had in my life. My favorite things

I have always loved to travel and discover new cultures. I have had the opportunity to travel to all sorts of places, like South Korea and recently Tanzania, which have allowed me to have a deeper understanding of the different cultures that there are around the world. It also taught me how to appriciate the different methods and customs that are so different from my day to day life. I want to continue traveling until I die. I also love food.
