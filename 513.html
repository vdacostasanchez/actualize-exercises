Life Potion

Recipe to be young again

Ingredients

  • 1 large cauldron
  • 2 drops oil of boil
  • 1 dead man's toe
  • 1 dab newt saliva
  • 1 dash of pox

Directions

  1. Bringeth to a full rousing bubble, then add two drops oil of boil and a dead man's toe.
  2. Add a dab of newt saliva, dash of pox. Stir thrice.
  3. One final thing and all is done, add a piece of thine own tongue.
  4. Administer one drop to victim and stand back. When the victim's life force shows, inhale the aura.
  5. When the aura is estinguished, the victim is dead and you shall be young again!
